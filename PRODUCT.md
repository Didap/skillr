# PRODUCT.md — Skillr

## Product Purpose
Skillr is the "Tinder for work": a rapid, double-opt-in matching platform between professionals and companies. It eliminates the friction of traditional job boards by focusing on total transparency and zero-time-wasted interactions.

## Users
1. **Professionals**: Students, freelancers, and full-time workers looking for their next gig or career move. They value speed, rate transparency, and verified company seriousness.
2. **Companies**: Startups and established firms looking for talent (React devs, designers, PMs, etc.). They value VAT-verified partners and quick call booking without chat loops.

## Brand Tone
- **Direct & Transparent**: No "corporate speak." Clear rates, clear expectations.
- **Dynamic & Fluid**: Fast-paced, interaction-heavy (swipe), mobile-first.
- **Premium & Serious**: Minimalist but high-fidelity. It's not a toy; it's a tool for high-value connections.
- **Emerald/Slate Palette**: Fresh yet professional.

## Strategic Principles
1. **Total Transparency**: Salary/Rate is always visible and mandatory.
2. **No Chat**: Deliberate product choice. Match -> Book Call. No messaging noise.
3. **Double Opt-in**: Safety and mutual interest by default.
4. **Verified Trust**: VAT verification for companies is mandatory.
